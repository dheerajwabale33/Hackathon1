Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about HealthGuard.

How accurate are the health measurements? +

HealthGuard uses medical-grade sensors that have been clinically validated. The heart rate monitor is accurate to within ±2 bpm, and the ECG app can detect atrial fibrillation with 99% accuracy. SpO₂ measurements are accurate to within ±2%.

How long does the battery last? +

The Standard model offers up to 5 days of battery life with typical use. The Advanced and Premium models feature larger batteries that can last up to 7 days. Battery life varies based on usage of GPS and continuous health monitoring features.

Is HealthGuard waterproof? +

Yes, all HealthGuard models are rated IP68 for water resistance, meaning they can withstand immersion in up to 1.5 meters of water for 30 minutes. You can wear it while swimming, showering, or in the rain.

How does the SOS feature work? +

When you press and hold the SOS button for 3 seconds, HealthGuard will first vibrate as a warning. If you continue holding, it will send an alert to your emergency contacts with your precise GPS location. It will also call the first contact on your list if they don't respond to the alert within 60 seconds.

Can I share health data with my doctor? +

Yes, the HealthGuard app allows you to generate detailed health reports that you can share with healthcare providers. These reports include trends in your heart rate, blood oxygen, ECG readings, and activity levels over selected time periods.
